I am Sabib Ahmed, a Computer Science graduate from Sylhet Engineering College (affiliated with SUST, Bangladesh). You can reach me at sabibahmed40@gmail.com.

I am seeking Ph.D. opportunities to advance interpretable, robust, and data-efficient AI, with a focus on medical imaging, generative models, and real-time visual detection systems. My research interests lie at the intersection of computer vision, explainable AI, multimodal learning, and trustworthy deep learning for clinically and societally impactful applications. I aim to develop deployable AI frameworks that integrate transformer architectures, generative modeling, and interpretable learning for real-world healthcare and autonomous systems.

📣📣📣 I am actively seeking Ph.D. opportunities for Spring 2027/Fall 2027.
